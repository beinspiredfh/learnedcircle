Audit trail

Record who changed what, when, and why.

Admin approvals, rejections, archived records and payment status changes should be stored in admin_audit_log. Run the audit migration once in Supabase before relying on this record for compliance.

Each moderation decision records item type, item id, status and reviewer note. Each payment decision records payment id, prior status, new status and admin note. Export records should include moderation, payment and audit data for backup.

Payment workflow

How admin should handle payments before full automation.

Confirm payment reference, match it to the user or work request, update the status, then record the final action. For client-lawyer work, the lawyer quote should show the gross fee, LearnedCircle commission and the lawyer settlement amount before payment is requested.

1. Client requests paid work from a lawyer profile 2. Lawyer sends fee quote through the platform 3. Client pays through LearnedCircle 4. Platform records 5% commission 5. Admin marks lawyer settlement when paid 6. Disputes are held until reviewed

Settlement tracking

Client payment to lawyer settlement.

Every paid legal work request should have a visible audit trail: client payment, LearnedCircle commission, lawyer net settlement, settlement status and dispute status where applicable.

01 Client paid

Confirm the Paystack reference and gross lawyer fee.

02 5% commission

Record LearnedCircle's platform commission automatically or manually.

03 Settlement pending

Hold the lawyer net amount until the work status is clear.

04 Lawyer paid

Mark settlement paid, held or disputed with an admin note.
