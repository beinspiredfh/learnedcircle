Risk and Mitigation

The main risks are known and manageable.

Investors expect risk. The strength of LearnedCircle is that the most obvious risks can be reduced with verification, moderation, clear terms, careful payment flows and focused operations.

Risk What could happen How LearnedCircle reduces it
Fake or weak profiles Unverified users may create misleading lawyer profiles. SCN collection, year of call, admin verification, reporting tools and public verification status.
Legal liability Users may treat platform content as legal advice from LearnedCircle. Terms, privacy notice, disclaimer, participant undertaking and clear separation between platform and lawyer advice.
Moderation burden Draft posts, adverts and profiles may require review. Admin moderation queue, premium publishing rules, report profile workflow and content standards.
Slow paid conversion Some lawyers may use free accounts without upgrading. Premium benefits include direct contact, visibility, online chat, faster publishing and unlimited branded invoices.
