About LearnedCircle

A legal help, publishing and opportunity network.

LearnedCircle helps clients discover lawyers, submit legal briefs, contact premium lawyers directly, read professional legal insight, find jobs and opportunities, and follow senior legal commentary. Lawyers can build public profiles, publish articles, mentor younger lawyers and connect with other verified lawyers.

The platform is owned by ADVOCATI LP and is operated as a directory, classifieds, moderation and networking platform. It does not itself provide legal advice or representation.

Contact

Support and moderation contact.

For platform support, moderation reports, guest blog submissions, lawyer verification questions or advertising requests, contact LearnedCircle at learnedcircle@gmail.com until a dedicated support mailbox is launched.

Urgent profile complaints should include the profile name, URL or screenshot, the reason for the report and contact information for follow-up.

Lawyer Verification Policy

What verification means.

Verification means LearnedCircle has reviewed the submitted lawyer profile for basic completeness and professional plausibility, including SCN, year of call, identity signals, location, practice areas, profile summary and public display preferences. Verification is not a guarantee of competence, availability, standing with any regulator, outcome or suitability for a particular matter.

  • SCN is required for lawyer profile submission.
  • Year of call and call details may be reviewed privately and shown publicly only where the lawyer opts in.
  • Profiles with vague, misleading, unverifiable or overly promotional claims may be rejected.
  • Verified profiles can still be suspended, corrected or removed if new concerns arise.

Advertise With Us

Free and paid adverts are reviewed.

LearnedCircle supports free reviewed adverts and paid priority adverts. Paid adverts can receive stronger visibility, clearer placement and more reliable campaign support, but they must still comply with legal, professional, ethical and platform rules.

  • Adverts must be lawful, accurate and relevant to a legal, professional, business or education audience.
  • Paid placement does not mean endorsement by LearnedCircle or ADVOCATI LP.
  • Misleading guarantees, unlawful solicitation, impersonation and abusive content are prohibited.
  • Admin may reject, label, edit, pause or remove adverts for safety, quality or legal reasons.

Guest Blog Publishing Policy

Senior legal voices are published by admin only.

Guest blog articles from SANs, retired judges, senior lawyers, academics and respected legal professionals are published by the LearnedCircle editor from the admin desk. Each guest article should include the approved title, contributor name, designation, writer picture where available, approved byline, summary and article body.

Guest articles remain informational commentary. They are not legal advice by LearnedCircle and should not disclose confidential client information or pending matter details without proper authority.

Takedown and Report Handling

Reports are reviewed and recorded.

LearnedCircle may review reports about profiles, articles, adverts, jobs, messages and forum posts. Depending on the issue, admin may request more information, correct content, add a note, restrict visibility, suspend a feature, reject a draft, archive a listing or remove content.

  • High-risk reports involving impersonation, confidentiality, fraud or unlawful content should be treated first.
  • Reviewer notes should record the reason for approval, rejection, archive or removal.
  • Users may be asked to provide supporting information before content is restored or approved.
