Lawyer Verification Checklist

Approve only complete, credible profiles.

  • SCN supplied and formatted consistently.
  • Year of call supplied and plausible for the stated experience.
  • Full name, public display name, location and Nigerian state/FCT selected.
  • Practice areas match the profile summary and are not misleading.
  • Public/private call-detail option is respected.
  • Profile picture, if supplied, looks professional and does not impersonate another person.
  • Profile summary avoids guarantees, confidential facts and unverifiable claims.
  • Pro bono, mentorship and premium visibility flags are intentional.

Profile Rejection Reasons

Use clear reviewer notes.

  • SCN missing or inconsistent with submitted name.
  • Incomplete location, practice areas or professional summary.
  • Claims of guaranteed case outcomes or misleading expertise.
  • Possible impersonation, duplicate profile or unverifiable identity.
  • Confidential client information included in the public profile.
  • Unprofessional profile picture, abusive language or spam content.

Advert Approval Rules

Free and paid adverts must remain credible.

  • Confirm the advertiser name, organization, contact details and target audience.
  • Reject unlawful, misleading, defamatory, discriminatory or scam-like adverts.
  • Reject adverts promising legal outcomes, court influence or guaranteed immigration/tax results.
  • Label paid adverts clearly and keep them relevant to the legal/professional audience.
  • Paid adverts get stronger visibility only after payment confirmation.

Guest Blog Publishing Rules

Admin-only publishing for senior contributors.

  • Confirm contributor name, title, designation and approved byline before publishing.
  • Add writer picture URL only where permission is clear.
  • Review article for confidentiality, defamation, pending-matter risk and excessive promotion.
  • Keep editorial note and contributor byline visible under the article card.
  • Archive drafts that are not approved or that require contributor clarification.

Takedown and Report Handling

Prioritize high-risk reports first.

  • Handle impersonation, fraud, confidentiality breaches and unlawful content urgently.
  • Preserve moderation records before removing public content.
  • Use reviewer notes to record what was reported, the evidence received and the action taken.
  • When unsure, temporarily archive the content pending clarification.
  • Escalate professional misconduct or regulatory issues to qualified counsel before final action.

Seed Content

Current launch content targets.

  • 5 to 10 sample lawyer profiles across corporate, tax, family, human rights, IP, banking and oil and gas.
  • At least 3 legal news/editor picks for courts, regulatory and tax updates.
  • At least 3 articles for client education and lawyer publishing examples.
  • At least 2 senior guest blog placeholders for SANs, retired judges or senior lawyers.
  • At least 3 job or opportunity samples across remote, in-house, retainer and pro bono work.

Payment Readiness

Plans to connect when payment account is ready.

  • Lawyer Premium Monthly: N10,000 per month.
  • Lawyer Premium Yearly: N100,000 per year.
  • Employer Job Post: per-post fee to be confirmed before activation.
  • Paid Advert: priority placement fee to be confirmed by campaign duration.
  • Featured Lawyer Placement: premium-only visibility add-on.

User Journey Test Checklist

Test these before each launch push.

  • Client submits a case brief and matched lawyers appear premium-first.
  • Client searches lawyers and sends a direct message without registration.
  • Lawyer signs up, accepts terms and submits SCN-required profile details.
  • Admin approves lawyer profile and verifies it appears in the directory.
  • Premium lawyer appears in direct-contact and online visibility surfaces.
  • Advertiser submits free and paid advert options for moderation.
  • Admin publishes guest blog with picture URL, writer byline and summary.
  • Report profile form sends moderation notification and appears in the queue.
